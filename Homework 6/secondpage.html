Short Story


Once upon a time there were three little piggies

Two of these pigs weren''t very smart, but their eldest brother actually had intelligence

One day, a hungry wolf came along looking to snack on the three piggies

To save their hides, each of the pigs built a house to defend themselves against the wolf

Unfortunately, the two not smart pigs built structurally unsound houses, which fell quite easily, and got them eaten

However, still alive, the eldest pig actually built a house out of brick, proving why he was the only intelligent one

Unfortunately for the wolf, the house was too strong to knock down, so he couldn't eat the pig

Stupidly, the wolf thought it best to sneak through the chimney, where he would fall into a boiling pot of water and die

After the wolf died, the eldest pig lived happily ever after, despite the fact that both of his brothers were dead, but oh well.
